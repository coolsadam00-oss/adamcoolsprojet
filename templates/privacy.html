Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

This policy explains what {{ site_name }} collects and how it is used.

Information We Collect

If you create an account, we collect your email address, password hash, verification status, and login information needed to keep you signed in. If you use Google sign-in, we may receive your email address, display name, and profile picture from Google.

If an admin uploads a game, we store the game title, description, tags, thumbnail, uploaded files, and upload time.

How We Use Information

We use account information to sign you in, verify your email, show admin access, protect the site, and contact you about your account or uploaded content.

Email Verification

When you sign up with email and password, we send a verification link to the email address you entered. If email sending is not configured, the verification link may appear in server logs for setup testing.

Cookies

The site uses a session cookie to keep you signed in. Your browser may also store theme preferences.

Children

This site is not for children under 13. Do not create an account if you are under 13. If the site owner learns that an account belongs to a child under 13, the account may be removed.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information if needed to run the site, follow the law, respond to a valid complaint, or protect the site and its users.

Your Choices

You can browse games as a guest. To request account deletion or removal of uploaded content, contact the site owner.

Security

Passwords are stored as hashes. No website can promise perfect security, so use a strong password that you do not reuse elsewhere.
